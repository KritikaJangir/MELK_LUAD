# SIGNOR Database Information

- **Full Database Name:** SIGNOR (SIGnaling Network Open Resource)
- **Version Used:** SIGNOR 3.0 (2024 Release)
- **Access Date(s):** 21-10-2025
- **Persistent Identifier/URL:** https://signor.uniroma2.it/
- **Description of Data Used:** Used to retrieve causal signaling relationships, including directed interactions (activation/inhibition), pathway-level regulatory networks, and curated mechanistic connections relevant to LUAD-associated genes.
- **Key Parameters/Filters Applied:** Homo sapiens selection, causal interaction type (activation/inhibition), confidence score, pathway-specific filtering, and extraction of regulatory subnetworks.
